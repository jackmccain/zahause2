The Zuhause Project

A solution to Vienna's housing problems that puts socioeconomic and racial equality first.

Introducing The Zuhause Project, a 501(c)(3)-backed project to unite the people of Vienna in the fight for a housing & city development approach that encourages integration, reduces the wealth gap, and promotes socioeconomic equality.

Zuhause : "at home" (German)

Our mission

to unite the people of Vienna in the fight for a housing & city development approach that encourages integration, reduces the wealth gap, and promotes socioeconomic equality.

Our work at Zuhause is to bring researchers, citizens, and student and community leaders together. We are a hub for prominent research & journal publications, manage and operate a data dashboard that offers deep insights into Vienna's cultural makeup and migration patterns. We track diversity and quantify it with population statistics across 243 distinct areas / communities in Vienna, Austria.
